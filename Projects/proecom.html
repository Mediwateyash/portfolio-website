E-Commerce Website for Sneakers

Project Overview

Sharky is a front-end e-commerce website designed to bridge the gap between local shop owners and online customers. Built using HTML, JavaScript, and CSS, this platform provides an intuitive and straightforward interface for purchasing sneakers. The website emphasizes simplicity and ease of use, integrating WhatsApp for customer communication and transactions, making it ideal for small businesses and local stores.

Project Details

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Features

  • Homepage: Displays an attractive array of sneakers with options to browse by popular brands like Nike, Adidas, Puma, Reebok, and Tommy Hilfiger.
  • Product Sections: Each brand section contains 8 sneakers, complete with pricing and a "Buy Now" button.
  • Header Navigation: Includes company links and a customer care button that redirects users to WhatsApp for direct support.
  • Advertisements: Scrolling down reveals promotional banners for sales and special offers.
  • Footer: Contains explore options, support options (Product Help, Customer Care, Authorized Services), and social media links (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn).
  • WhatsApp Integration: Clicking "Buy Now" or "Customer Care" redirects users to WhatsApp, where they can inquire about products or seek support. This approach avoids convenience fees associated with online banking.

Purpose and Goals

  • Simplicity: The website is designed to be user-friendly, facilitating quick purchases and direct communication.
  • Local Business Support: Sharky Sharky aims to help local shop owners reach a broader audience by providing an online platform that is both accessible and easy to use.
  • Cost Efficiency: By using WhatsApp for transactions and customer support, the website minimizes additional costs for users, making it more appealing for small-scale operations.
